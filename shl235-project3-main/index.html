A visionary, a designer, an entrepeneuer, and much more

Who?

Pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s- 1980s.

Forever changed how we interact with technology in our daily lives.

Transformed Apple.Inc into a front runner in telecommunications.

What?

Welcomed the world into the new way of working with technology.

Had a vision for a company that would create technology for the world that would allow users to quickly get tasks done, and to do so with ease.

Inspired people and companies around the world with the idea of minimalism that was not only present in his products, but also his approach to life.
